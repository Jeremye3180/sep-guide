Everything you need to know about portfolios?

Why do we have porfolios in SEP?

Portfolios archive your work that you have done in class so projects are eaiser to find. It also represents a huge part of your future career in software because compainies like google want to see what you are capable of in your projects.



How to make an efficient, Meaningful Pofolio for Your work!

In order to make the most out of your portfolio, you should include, your embedded project, a way to see your code, and a MEANINGFUL reflection on the project.

Embedding your project



Compainies want to see what you have created without going out of their way to find it on github

Showing your code!



Why show our code if it allows others to use it in their own work?



We show our code because we want others to see how we accomplished a certain aspect of our project! This way, students can learn from eachother's code and implement their findings in their own projects!



Your reflection



This is where students crtique themselves on the project they have just completed. A reflection is meant to inform viewers about the purpose of the project, how the student came up with it, challenges that they faced, and their next steps.

Your Entry Tittle



Lastly,do not forget to create a title for each entry that applies to the corrosponding project!
